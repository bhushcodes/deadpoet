Last Year

No one was seen me,
Last year…!
Fake peace of all time,
Last year…!

Why year starts,
With no peace,
No happiness,
Why does it always start…!

Last year…!
I was last of peace,
Last year…!

It started with,
No peace again,
No happiness,
This year…!

This year is,
Same as,
Last year…!
